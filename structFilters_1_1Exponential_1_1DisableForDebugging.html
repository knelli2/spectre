template<size_t FilterIndex>
struct Filters::Exponential< FilterIndex >::DisableForDebugging

Turn the filter off.

This option exists to temporarily disable the filter for debugging purposes. For problems where filtering is not needed, the preferred approach is to not compile the filter into the executable.
