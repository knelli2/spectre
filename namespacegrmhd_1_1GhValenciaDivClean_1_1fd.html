Reconstructs \(\rho, p, Wv^i, B^i\), \(\Phi\), the spacetime metric, \(\Phi_{iab}\), and \(\Pi_{ab}\), then computes the Lorentz factor, upper spatial velocity, specific internal energy, specific enthalpy, and the conserved variables. All results are written into vars_on_face.

This is used on DG elements to reconstruct their subcell neighbors' solution on the shared faces.
